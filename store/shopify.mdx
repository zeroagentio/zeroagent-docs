Zeroagent simplifies the integration of AI-powered assistants into your Shopify Online Store or any custom eCommerce platform. Below are the steps to connect and configure Zeroagent for both Shopify and Custom Stores.

## Connecting Shopify 

<Steps>
  <Step title="🛠️ Select site">
    Click `add site` and choose `shopify`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="🔑 Authorization">
    You will be redirected to the store's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="🗂️ Knowledge Base">
    All your `products` are added default to AI Knowledge.
    <Note>
    Learn more about the [Knowledge Base](/knowledge-base).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="⚙️ Chat Setting">
    Update your chat widget interface settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="✅ AI Store Assistant is Live">
    Your AI Store Assistant is live and ready to assist customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>


<Warning>
If your store is disconnected from zeroagent platform, you will be notified to re-connect store.
</Warning>
