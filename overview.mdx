# ZeroAgent: Your Personalized Business Agents 

Imagine having a team of expert assistants, each specialized in a specific business app, working tirelessly to streamline your workflows. ZeroAgent makes this a reality. 
With zeroagent, you can:

### 🔌 Connect with 10+ Apps
  Easily integrate AI Agent with GitHub, Google Calendar, Slack, and more! Streamline your workflow by letting AI manage your daily tasks.
### 🤖 **Automate Tasks** 
 Use simple chat prompts to create GitHub repos or schedule meetings—your AI agent handles it all!
### 🧑🏻 **Human-in-the-Loop**
Ensure accuracy and control with our human-in-the-loop feature! Get a confirmation prompt for critical actions and proceed only after approval.
### ⚙️ **Quick Setup** 
Authorize access in a few steps, and your AI agent is ready to work. Focus on what matters while AI takes care of routine tasks.
### 🎯 **Personalized for You** 
 Customize the AI to fit your workflow, providing smart suggestions and fast execution based on your preferences.


# 🛠️ Tool Category
Here are the major categories of apps you can integrate

<AccordionGroup>

 <Accordion title="Version Control & Code Management">
    Apps like `GitHub`, `Gitlab` allow you to manage repositories, pull requests, and version control tasks via simple prompts.
  </Accordion>

 <Accordion title="Communication & Collaboration">
    Integrate with `Slack`, `Discord`, or `Zoom` to streamline team conversations, set reminders, or manage meetings.
  </Accordion>

 <Accordion title="Calendar & Scheduling">
    Apps like `Calendly` help you schedule, modify, and manage events across your team’s calendar using natural language commands.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cloud Hosting & Databases">
 Manage database by integrating with `Airtable`, or host your application through `Netlify`.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Project & Task Management" >
   Connect with platforms like `Notion` or `Todoist` to automate task creation, project updates, and task tracking effortlessly.
  </Accordion>


  <Accordion title="CRM" >
   Manage customer relationships with apps like `Capsule` plus many more CRMs.
  </Accordion>

 
</AccordionGroup>
