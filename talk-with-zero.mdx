**Talk with Zero** is an AI assistant designed to make managing your online store effortless. Acting as an **AI Store Manager**, it handles various tasks like processing orders, tracking inventory, and offering valuable insights into customer activity and store performance—freeing you to focus on growing your business.

# Features

#### **1. Show Uploaded Files 📂**: 
- 📄 Access all uploaded files directly through the interface.
- 📁 Simplifies managing your store’s resources by organizing and displaying files for quick reference.

#### 2. Manage Your Chat Settings 💬
- ✨ Customize your chat widget settings to suit your store’s needs.
- 🎨 Adjust appearance, behavior, and responses to align with your brand.

#### 3. View & Manage User Tickets 🎟️
- 📋 Keep track of customer issues and inquiries through a centralized ticketing system.
- 🛠️ Resolve tickets efficiently, ensuring a smooth customer support experience.

#### 4. View Collections 🛍️
- 🗂️ Browse and manage product collections in your store with ease.
- ✅ Ensure your product catalog is well-organized and up-to-date.

#### 5. Manage Inventory 🔍
- ⚡ Quickly find & manage products in your store using smart search functionality.
- 🕒 Saves time and ensures you can access product details instantly.

#### 6. Search for Customers 🧑‍🤝‍🧑
- 🔎 Look up customer information with powerful search tools.
- 🛒 Access order history, contact details, and preferences to deliver personalized service.

# Benefits of Using Talk with Zero

- **Efficiency** 🚀: Automates routine tasks to save time.
- **Personalization** 🎯: Offers tailored interactions for your shoppers.
- **Insightful** 📊: Provides actionable insights to improve store performance.
- **Customer Satisfaction** ❤️: Ensures fast and efficient customer support.

Talk with Zero empowers store owners by combining advanced AI with easy-to-use features, helping you run your online store like a pro.
